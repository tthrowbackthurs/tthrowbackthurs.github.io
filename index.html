Historical Background

In 1966, just over 6.3 million students were enrolled in college; by the 2017 school year, more than 19.5 million students were enrolled. This increase has also seen a large uptick in tuition and student loans. This week, we're exploring the trends in tuition across different institution types.

We'd really love to see what you can do with this week's dataset! Download the data and see what you can visualize.
