Historical Background

A really quick write up this week. Coming out of World War II, the race for military dominance had shifted to space. The United States and the Soviet Union began a series of space missions that would officially be kicked off by the Soviet's launch of Sputnik and culminated with the landing of Apollo 11 on the Moon. This week's dataset looks at a subset of space history, focusing on manned space missions.
