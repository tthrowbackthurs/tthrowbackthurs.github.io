US Federal Budget by Department - 1962 to 2018

2019 Week 13 Viz

Dataviz Approach

When I first looked at this data, I brought it into Tableau as a quick area chart. What immediately jumped out at me was that the bulk of the budget allocations fell within 4 or so departments. So, I decided to revisit the Kevin Flerlage No Polygons method to show the allocations across the top 4 departments, and all others, by year. Combine that with small multiples broken out into rows of 4 years to correspond with presidential terms.

To add a bit more information, I used a couple really simple viz in tooltips. The first is a break out of budgeted outlay for the top 4 departments, and the second was a bar chart of all the year's total budgeted dollars (inflation adjusted). The thing that sets this one apart from others is the use of a set action to highlight the year that the user is hovering over. This makes it super easy for the viewer to compare the budget across different fiscal years.
